Typhoon Safety Guide

Before the Storm
  • Monitor weather updates via PAGASA.
  • Charge all electronics and power banks.
  • Complete your Go-Bag using our Kit Builder.
  • Secure loose items outside your home.
During the Storm
  • Stay indoors and away from windows.
  • Turn off main electricity if flooding is imminent.
  • Rely on battery-powered radios for news.
  • Do not wade in floodwaters.
After the Storm
  • Wait for official clearance before going out.
  • Check for structural damage or gas leaks.
  • Boil tap water before drinking.
  • Report fallen power lines to Meralco.
